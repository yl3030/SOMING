The Peloton is a DB alloy urban bike with an alloy fork, 2X8 SHIMANO CLARIS shifting and rim brakes. This bike is most suitable for riding distances of 15-40km. It has the shortest wheelbase in its class, and the short wheelbase and rigid frame combine to create a bike that has nimble handling and a very enjoyable ride.

The Cadence is an all-round training bike with a butted aluminum alloy frame and carbon fiber fork. It is also equipped with a SHIMANO TIAGRA 2X10 derailleur system and disc brake. Its well-balanced performance makes it a great fit for many different kinds of riders, including climbers, endurance riders and triathletes. It is also well-suited for racing and travelling.
