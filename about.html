ABOUT SOMINGS

Somings has moved the headquartered from London, England to Fukuoka, Japan. The Somings team is made up of professionals, cyclists, and engineers. We use a cloud office system, and the connecting Somings team spans the globe. Ours team works endlessly every day to create stylish, simple, and expressive lifestyle bikes that make the journey as fun as the destination.

We Make Bikes to Fit Different Riders

The core tenet of Somings is a belief in the transformative ability of bikes to make lifestyle changes more enjoyable, and make the planet a better place. Colourful bikes are just the beginning — soon Somings will create a range of lifestyle-related products as well. Join us on our journey.

ABOUT RIKULAU

Rikulau - the legendary sacred animal of the Rukai people (a Taiwanese aboriginal tribe). Rikulau is the name of clouded leopard in their language.The boutique bicycle brand named RIKULAU has a world-class reputation for customized top titanium alloy and stainless steel frames. The brand spirit symbolizes challenges, travel and adventure.
