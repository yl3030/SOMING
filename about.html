ABOUT SOMINGS

Make your Life more Colorful.

SOMINGS was established in London, England in 2018. In order to focus on the Asian market, it has relocated its headquarter to Fukuoka City, Japan. SOMINGS has always advocated that cycling should be freer whether the cyclists are professional players or amateurs.The more complex the world, the more people desire simplicity. The same applies to cycling. Therefore, the bicycle brand of SOMINGS simplifies the maintenance process by strengthening the durability and pays more attention to the design of the appearance. The well-designed color scheme sets off a unique style and enables cyclists to show personal bicycle style in each ride!

The joy of riding originates from interactions with the environment, as well as the riding experiences brought by different bicycle models. We believe Somings bikes can help people exercise and improve their lifestyle while reducing their carbon footprint. Along the way, the Somings team created a planet-friendly manufacturing process. This process reduces waste while using locally recyclable materials and practicing environmental protection.

We Make Bikes to Fit Different Riders

According to the needs of different consumer groups, SOMINGS adheres to rigorous R&D spirit and technology in product development by targeting at functional requirements of mechanical or hydraulic disc brakes, wheelbase arrangements, and transmission systems. It also performs comprehensive assessments on user needs, such as riding surface and riding distance. SOMINGS and its partner RIKULAU work together to create bicycle models that meet various riding needs, making every ride an enjoyment.

ABOUT RIKULAU

RIKULAU means "clouded leopard" in the Drekay language of indigenous peoples in Taiwan. Legend has it that the Taiwanese clouded leopard is the ancestral spirit of the Drekay tribe, leading their ancestors to climb over the mountains and find a peaceful land for dwelling. The boutique bicycle brand in the name of RIKULAU was created in 2006, with the dream of turning the legendary figure of the clouded leopard into a bicycle. RIKULAU is proud of its manufacturing process and has a world-class reputation for customized top grade titanium alloy and stainless steel frames.
